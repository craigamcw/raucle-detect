commercial — see [LICENSING.md](./LICENSING.md)). For that model to
work, epic28 Ltd (trading as Raucle) must be able to offer **all** of the project's code —
including community contributions — under both the AGPL and the
commercial licence. This CLA grants the rights that make that possible,
**without taking your rights away**: you keep full ownership of your
contribution and may use it however you like elsewhere.

Every contribution also requires a [DCO](./DCO) sign-off
(`git commit -s`), which certifies you have the right to submit it. The
DCO certifies *origin*; this CLA grants the *licence* Raucle needs to
dual-licence. Both apply.
