# Commercial licensing

raucle-detect is dual-licensed. The default is AGPL-3.0-or-later (see [LICENSE](LICENSE) and [LICENSING.md](LICENSING.md)). For organisations whose use of raucle-detect is not compatible with AGPL terms, a commercial licence is available.

## Who needs a commercial licence

You need a commercial licence if any of the following apply:

1. **You embed raucle-detect inside a closed-source product** that you distribute, license, or offer as a service to third parties, and you are not prepared to release that product's entire source code under AGPL-3.0.
2. **You operate a hosted service (SaaS) that uses raucle-detect** to provide functionality to third-party users (whether free or paid), and you are not prepared to release the service's entire codebase under AGPL-3.0.
3. **You require warranties, indemnification, or SLA-backed support** that the open-source AGPL release does not provide.
4. **Your organisation's legal or procurement process specifically forbids AGPL-licensed dependencies.** Some regulated industries and some defence contractors have blanket bans on copyleft licences; we are happy to provide commercial-licensed copies.

## Who does NOT need a commercial licence

- **Self-hosting inside a single organisation for internal use.** Banks, fintechs, government departments, and similar that run raucle-detect on their own infrastructure to protect their own AI agents — without exposing it as a service to third parties — are fully covered by the AGPL.
- **Academic research, education, security audits, personal projects.** All free under AGPL.
- **Contributors to raucle-detect itself.** Contributing fixes, features, or extensions to the open-source repository is encouraged and requires no commercial licence.
- **Distributing raucle-detect alongside other AGPL-compatible software.** Including in Linux distributions, security platforms released under AGPL, etc.

## What a commercial licence includes

- A perpetual, non-exclusive licence to use raucle-detect inside your products without AGPL obligations.
- Optional: commercial support, including security patches, integration help, and a defined response-time SLA.
- Optional: indemnification against third-party intellectual-property claims.

Specific terms — including territory, deployment counts, and pricing — depend on your use case.

## How to enquire

Email **`commercial@raucle.com`** with a short description of your intended use:

- What product or service raucle-detect will be embedded in.
- Approximate scale (deployments, request volume per month, or seats).
- Any specific compliance requirements (FCA, BaFin, EU AI Act, ISO 27001, SOC 2, FedRAMP, etc.).

A quote and licence agreement will follow within five business days.
